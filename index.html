Teacher Rising
Practitioner-led. Classroom-proven.

Teacher Rising brings professional development built from the inside out — not from a textbook down. Every workshop, every session, every conversation is grounded in a decade of real classroom experience and the belief that when teachers grow, students flourish.

What We Believe

  • Young people are generally curious and want to learn. If put in the right situation, learning will occur.
  • Teaching is about building relationships, and leveraging them to get students to take academic risks and to respect the classroom's goals and expectations.
  • Our education system was built for a world that no longer exists. Working within it and innovating within it are not opposites — both are required.
  • Teaching is a craft. It needs to be sharpened and re-sharpened. Quality instruction raises output and results.

Professional Development

Teach to Every Mind

4.5 Act 48 Hours

Three strategies for reaching every learner: "Inverted Media" hooks that activate prior knowledge before a text, metacognitive think-aloud modeling that makes the reading process visible, and multi-modal text sets with non-worksheet assessments. Participants leave with a complete, classroom-ready lesson using all three.

Understanding AI & Practical Tools for Teachers

5 Act 48 Hours

A hands-on workshop covering how LLMs actually work, live practice with Brisk Teaching (text leveling, differentiated assessments) and Curipod (interactive lesson builder), and guided setup of Claude Code for creating custom classroom tools, review chatbots, and administrative tasks. Every participant leaves with a real, finished deliverable.

Cybersecurity for the School & Classroom

4.5 Act 48 Hours

Built for teachers and staff — not IT departments. Participants work through the Google Phishing Quiz, learn FERPA basics and student data obligations, set up MFA on school accounts, and complete a simulated ransomware breach exercise that puts response protocols into practice in real time.

1-on-1 Mentoring

New teachers don't just need PD — they need a guide. Teacher Rising offers personalized 1-on-1 mentoring for first-year teachers, providing ongoing support through the real challenges of the classroom: building culture from day one, navigating difficult relationships, maintaining high expectations, and developing the confidence to see it through. This isn't coaching from a manual — it's mentorship from someone who's been in those rooms.
