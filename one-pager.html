Teacher Rising
Practitioner-led. Classroom-proven.

Teacher Rising brings professional development built from the ground up — not from a textbook down. Every workshop, session, and conversation is grounded in over a decade of real classroom experience and the belief that with the right supports, students flourish.

What We Believe

  • Young people are generally curious and want to learn. If put in the right situation, learning will occur.
  • Teaching is about building relationships, and leveraging them to get students to take academic risks and to respect the classroom's goals and expectations.
  • Our education system was built for a world that no longer exists. Working within it and innovating within it are not opposites — both are required.
  • Teaching is a craft. It needs to be sharpened and re-sharpened. Quality instruction raises output and results.

Professional Development

Day 1 SurThrival Kit

Equip first-year and transitioning teachers with the mindset, tools, and strategies to not just survive their first days, but to set the tone for an entire year of high-expectation, relationship-driven learning.

Act 48 Approved

Teach to Every Mind

Three strategies for reaching every learner: "Inverted Media" hooks that activate prior knowledge before a text, metacognitive think-aloud modeling that makes the reading process visible, and multi-modal text sets with non-worksheet assessments. Participants leave with a complete, classroom-ready lesson using all three.

Act 48 Approved

Understanding AI & Practical Tools for Teachers

A hands-on workshop covering how LLMs actually work, live practice with Brisk Teaching (text leveling, differentiated assessments) and Curipod (interactive lesson builder), and guided setup of Claude Code for creating custom classroom tools, review chatbots, and administrative tasks. Every participant leaves with a real, finished deliverable.

Act 48 Approved

Cybersecurity for the School & Classroom

Don't be the next headline! Cyber safety training built for teachers and staff. Participants work through the Google Phishing Quiz, learn FERPA basics and student data obligations, discuss setting up MFA on school accounts, and complete a simulated ransomware breach exercise that puts response protocols into practice in real time.

1-on-1 Mentoring

New teachers don't just need PD — they need a guide. Teacher Rising offers personalized 1-on-1 mentoring for first-year teachers, providing ongoing support through the real challenges of the classroom: building culture from day one, navigating difficult relationships, maintaining high expectations, and developing the confidence to see it through.
